Voice?

Voice. My voice creates reality. Without reality, there is dissonance. I keep peace on this planet and I have, in my whole life kept it hidden to everyone else. But I was always known. Known for my voice that could show barrier between truth and fake.

I've protected this my whole life, only for me to be betrayed by my only dear universe. I've seen darkness corrupt my planet, and I'm seeing it corrupt my voice. But I won't let it happen.

As soon as this corruption crawls deep in my heart, I must break this voice before it turns reality to dissonance.
