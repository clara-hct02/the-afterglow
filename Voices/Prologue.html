PROLOGUE

My world has been corrupted, but they'll never take me.

I command this world to live by itself, with my voice to be protected by its lone protectors. In every voice shards shattered may give life to the universe I loved living in, creating all elements a world must possess.

As soon as this corruption crawls deep in my heart, I must break this voice before it turns reality to dissonance.

Please, protect my voice.
